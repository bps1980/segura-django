Executive Summary

The SAM.gov Tools platform is a robust automation system empowering federal contractors to find, filter, and respond to opportunities faster. By aggregating data from sam.gov, ebuy.gsa.gov, and related procurement portals, it streamlines the acquisition workflow for small businesses and primes.

  • CRM-style contractor profile management

Architecture & Technologies

  • Backend: Django, PostgreSQL, Celery
  • Frontend: React or Django Templates
  • Document Gen: Python-docx, PyPDF2, ReportLab
  • Email Engine: SMTP with DKIM and SPF

  • Tracking due dates, NAICS matches, size standards
  • Upload and preview proposal documents
