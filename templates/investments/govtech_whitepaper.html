GovTech Proposal Engine

Segura Mgmt – Whitepaper

1. Introduction

The GovTech Proposal Engine is a smart automation system designed to help startups, small businesses, and federal contractors streamline their bidding process for government contracts. By automating solicitation tracking, scope analysis, and document generation, it empowers non-experts to participate in the lucrative federal and state contracting ecosystems.

2. Purpose

The purpose of this whitepaper is to explain the architecture, capabilities, and roadmap of the GovTech Proposal Engine to potential users, partners, and investors.

3. Core Features

  • Daily scraping of new SAM.gov and NASA SEWP solicitations based on NAICS codes
  • AI-based scope analysis for determining fit
  • Preloaded proposal templates for Technical, Pricing, and Past Performance sections
  • PDF generation with support for compliance formatting
  • Email packaging and logging system for auto-submission

4. Architecture Overview

  • Backend: Django, PostgreSQL, Celery
  • Frontend: React.js, Bootstrap
  • Integrations: SAM.gov API, FBO scraper, PDFKit, OpenAI GPT
  • Security: Role-based access control, JWT authentication

5. Automation Workflow

  1. Fetch solicitations from public government sources
  2. Extract scope, deadline, and evaluation factors
  3. Generate tailored proposals using stored templates + user inputs
  4. Auto-submit proposals via email or government portals
  5. Track submissions and status from the admin dashboard

6. Use Cases

  • SBIR/STTR submissions
  • 5G and AI government initiatives
  • Software modernization bids
  • Environmental & logistics contracts
  • Cybersecurity proposals for DoD and DHS

7. Monetization Strategy

  • Monthly subscription tiers based on document output limits
  • White-labeled SaaS license for consultants and GovCon incubators
  • Marketplace for prewritten technical modules and resumes

8. Roadmap

  • Q2 2025: Release MVP to select government partners
  • Q3 2025: Launch proposal pack marketplace
  • Q4 2025: Partner with PTAC offices and SBA consultants
  • 2026: Scale to 1,000+ users and offer DoD DFARS compliance tools

9. Compliance & Security

All user data is encrypted and stored securely. Documents meet formatting standards outlined by FAR, DFARS, and DoD instructions. Submissions are archived for 5 years for audit purposes.

10. Conclusion

The GovTech Proposal Engine removes the guesswork and inefficiencies of preparing and submitting government proposals. It’s time to level the playing field for small businesses with automation.
