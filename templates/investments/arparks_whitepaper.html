Introduction

The AR Parks App aims to revolutionize how visitors experience National Parks and cultural sites by offering immersive Augmented Reality experiences through a mobile app. By blending physical exploration with digital engagement, AR Parks enhances tourism, education, and conservation awareness.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to bridge the gap between nature and technology. We aim to provide a modern, educational, and interactive experience for park visitors of all ages while supporting the National Park Service in its mission to protect and promote natural heritage.

Product Overview

The AR Parks App provides:

  • 3D overlays of historic landmarks, flora, and fauna
  • Real-time trail and wildlife info
  • Interactive park maps with AR guidance
  • Educational games for children and students

Features and Use Cases

  • Augmented Historical Tours: Visitors can view recreations of historic events in their original locations.
  • AR Wildlife Spotting: Educational overlays highlight animal habitats and conservation facts.
  • Gamified Exploration: Users can collect AR badges by visiting points of interest.
  • Accessible Mode: Visual/audio enhancements for disabled visitors.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React Native / Flutter
  • AR Engine: Unity3D + Vuforia / ARKit / ARCore
  • Backend: Django + PostgreSQL
  • Mapping: Mapbox or Google Maps API

Development Roadmap

  • Q2 2025 – MVP for 2 national parks
  • Q3 2025 – Expanded coverage to 10 parks, multilingual support
  • Q4 2025 – Launch educational and donation modules
  • 2026 – Government contracts and global expansion

Revenue Model

  • In-app purchases (custom avatars, upgrades)
  • Government and nonprofit contracts
  • Branded sponsorships (e.g., outdoor gear brands)
  • Subscription for premium content and guided tours

Partnership Opportunities

We are actively seeking collaborations with:

  • National Park Services
  • AR/VR development firms
  • Environmental nonprofits
  • Tourism boards and educational institutions

Team

The AR Parks App is a project under Segura Mgmt, led by Brennen Segura and supported by a team of experienced developers, AR specialists, and conservation advisors.
