MDAV (Manned Drone Aviation Vehicle) Whitepaper

Executive Summary

MDAV is a next-generation manned drone vehicle that offers a unique, compact, and efficient alternative for short- to mid-range air transportation. Designed with aviation-grade components and integrated safety systems, MDAV is poised to reshape urban and regional mobility.

Vision

To enable individuals and organizations to travel faster and more efficiently using autonomous aerial mobility while ensuring safety, affordability, and environmental sustainability.

Vehicle Design

  • Fuselage: Lightweight aviation-grade aluminum frame
  • Engines: Multiple aviation fuel-powered engines for vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL)
  • Cockpit: Reinforced glass dome with top and bottom visibility
  • Control System: AI-assisted flight computer with manual override
  • Safety: Emergency parachute deployment system and redundant fail-safe protocols

Technology

  • Real-time sensor data processing for flight optimization
  • GPS and LIDAR navigation for precision movement
  • AI-based decision making for weather and obstacle avoidance
  • Integrated cloud-based monitoring and maintenance logging

Use Cases

  • Urban commuting
  • Emergency response and medical transport
  • Business and private chartering
  • Military reconnaissance (modular model)

Business Model

MDAV will generate revenue through direct sales, leasing to transportation networks, and after-market services such as maintenance, software updates, and safety certifications.

Roadmap

  • Q1 2025 – Design finalization and prototyping
  • Q2 2025 – Test flights and FAA compliance review
  • Q3 2025 – Initial production and early adopter program
  • Q1 2026 – Commercial release and scale-up

Team

The MDAV team is composed of experts in aviation engineering, AI systems, regulatory compliance, and manufacturing logistics. Segura Mgmt provides the executive leadership, innovation ecosystem, and operational infrastructure.
