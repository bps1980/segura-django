Decentralized E-commerce for Local and Global Sellers

1. Executive Summary

CajunSea Goods is a decentralized blockchain-powered SuperStore built under Segura Mgmt. It allows vendors to sell products globally, manage storefronts, and accept crypto and fiat payments with transparent, blockchain-based tracking.

2. The Problem

Traditional e-commerce platforms charge high fees, restrict vendor flexibility, and lack support for blockchain settlements and real-time payments.

3. The Solution

CajunSea Goods offers a decentralized marketplace with instant withdrawals, crypto-native features, AI listing suggestions, and integrated third-party logistics support.
