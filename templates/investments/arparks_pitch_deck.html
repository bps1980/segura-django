Segura Mgmt Presents: AR Parks App

Augmented Reality Experiences for National Parks

Problem

Traditional park visits are passive, lack engaging information delivery, and miss opportunities to inspire digital-native generations.

Solution

The AR Parks App delivers immersive augmented reality (AR) experiences directly through visitors' smartphones, overlaying digital content onto real-world park environments.

Key Features

  • AR Wildlife Guide (real-time species overlays)
  • 3D Historical Reconstructions
  • Interactive Hiking Maps
  • Educational Mini-Games for Children
  • AR Selfie Spots with Park-Themed Filters

Market Opportunity

  • Over 330 million national park visits annually in the U.S.
  • Rising adoption of mobile AR applications
  • Government & tourism grant opportunities

Revenue Model

  • Freemium access + premium content upgrades
  • National park commissions or partnerships
  • Sponsorships from outdoor brands
  • Merch sales through in-app store

Technology Stack

  • Unity + AR Foundation
  • Geolocation APIs
  • Cloud Anchors & 3D Object Recognition
  • Firebase for backend user/session management

Current Stage

  • Prototype complete
  • User testing conducted in 2 parks
  • Seeking funding to scale to 10+ parks

Team

  • Brennen Segura – Founder / AR Visionary
  • Developer Team – Unity + UX Designers

Ask
