AI Chat

Segura Mgmt – Pitch Deck

1. Introduction

The AI Chat Assistant by Segura Mgmt is a next-generation conversational AI platform designed to automate support, sales, and internal operations across industries. Built using Flask, React, TensorFlow, and Hugging Face, it offers human-like, contextual interactions powered by advanced natural language understanding.

2. Problem

Traditional chatbot solutions offer limited understanding and rigid flows, leading to poor customer satisfaction, high churn, and over-reliance on human agents. Businesses need scalable, cost-efficient, and intelligent support solutions.

3. Solution

  • Deep NLP with sentiment, intent, and context retention
  • Real-time integration into websites, CRMs, and mobile apps
  • Custom domain training for legal, financial, healthcare, and retail industries
  • Secure data management and GDPR-ready deployments

4. Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React.js
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • ML/NLP: TensorFlow, Hugging Face Transformers
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Deployment: Docker + DigitalOcean

5. Market Opportunity

The global chatbot market is projected to exceed $10.5 billion by 2026. Our product is uniquely positioned for scalable adoption across e-commerce, insurance, healthcare, and finance by offering both plug-and-play and white-labeled deployments.

6. Revenue Model

  • SaaS subscriptions with tiered pricing
  • Enterprise licensing for private cloud and on-prem
  • Custom NLP/AI development for large clients
