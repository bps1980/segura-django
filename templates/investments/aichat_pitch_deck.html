AI Chat Assistant Pitch Deck

Introduction

The AI Chat Assistant by Segura Mgmt is a next-generation conversational platform built using Flask (Python backend), React (frontend), and TensorFlow for machine learning. Our goal is to revolutionize customer service and automation across sectors by delivering fast, intelligent, and context-aware chat interactions.

Problem

Businesses face increasing customer service demands while struggling to keep costs low. Traditional chatbots lack deep learning capabilities, resulting in limited automation and poor user experience.

Solution

Our AI Chat Assistant provides smart and responsive communication using natural language processing, sentiment analysis, and intent recognition. It seamlessly integrates into websites, CRMs, and mobile platforms.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React.js
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • Machine Learning: TensorFlow, Hugging Face Transformers
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Deployment: Docker + DigitalOcean

Market Opportunity

The global chatbot market is projected to reach $10.5 billion by 2026. Our AI Chat Assistant targets e-commerce, insurance, finance, and health sectors with customizable and scalable integrations.

Revenue Model

  • Enterprise Licensing
  • Custom Integrations
