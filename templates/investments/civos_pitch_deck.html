CIVOS: Civilization Operating System

By Segura Mgmt

AI-driven economic simulation platform to model, forecast, and stress-test civilization-scale economic reforms and future transitions.

Problem

  • Governments lack simulation tools to forecast the economic impact of major reforms like UBI, automation, or carbon taxation.
  • Traditional economic models are static, top-down, and disconnected from real-time data and behavior-driven outcomes.
  • Investors and institutions need a strategic planning tool for long-horizon macro trends.

Our Solution

CIVOS is a modular, AI-enhanced platform that models present and future economies using agent-based simulation, real-world data, and interactive policy levers.

  • Agent-based economic engine with custom citizen, corporate, and government actors
  • Scenario convergence slider to simulate transition over time
  • Visual dashboards comparing simulated vs. historical GDP, inflation, unemployment, and automation rates

Key Features

  • Agent-Based Modeling: Built on Python's Mesa for micro-to-macro simulation
  • AI-Powered Forecasting: GPT-based scenario reasoning & dynamic policy recommendations
  • Live Data Ingestion: FRED and World Bank datasets for real-world calibration
  • Interactive UI: Dash/Plotly dashboard with scenario stepping, graph comparisons, and export tools

Market Opportunity

  • GovTech: $20B+ global spend on public sector modernization and simulation tools
  • Economic Modeling & AI: $50B+ opportunity in think tanks, hedge funds, and policy labs
  • Digital Twin Software: Growing demand for nation-scale simulation and digital planning environments

Architecture Overview

  • Python + Mesa engine for agent simulation
  • FastAPI backend with policy scenario loader
  • Plotly Dash frontend with FRED-data overlay
  • Modular JSON-based scenario definitions and logging

Revenue Model

  • Think tank SaaS platform subscriptions
  • API access for macro hedge funds and research firms
  • Custom consulting engagements and model tuning
