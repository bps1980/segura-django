1. Introduction

CajunSea Goods is a decentralized multi-vendor marketplace designed to empower entrepreneurs and retailers through blockchain. Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, CajunSea leverages transparency, low fees, and crypto-native payments to bring innovation to both buyers and sellers.

2. Vision & Mission

Our mission is to become the premier decentralized marketplace where local brands meet global customers, all powered by Web3 infrastructure. We envision a future where trust is built through smart contracts and vendors operate without middlemen.

3. Platform Overview

CajunSea Goods enables vendors to register, manage their storefronts, list products, and process payments in both fiat and crypto. All transactions are logged on the blockchain, ensuring full traceability and trust.

4. Technology Stack

  • Frontend: JavaScript (React/Next.js)
  • Backend: Python (Django) + Node.js
  • Blockchain Layer: Solidity + Polygon/Ethereum integration
  • Payments: Stripe, Plaid, Coinbase Commerce
  • Hosting: DigitalOcean (Docker + Kubernetes)

5. Core Features

  • Multi-vendor onboarding with KYC/AML
  • Custom storefronts and product analytics
  • Smart contract-enabled transactions
  • Stablecoin support and fiat integration
  • Affiliate & Ambassador programs

6. Tokenomics

The CajunSea ecosystem is powered by the **CJN Token** (TBD if tokenized). Vendors can stake CJN for reduced fees, premium listing placement, and voting on governance proposals. Token distribution will follow:

  • 40%: Community & Ecosystem Growth
  • 25%: Founders & Team (Vested)
  • 20%: Strategic Investors
  • 10%: Marketing & Partnerships
  • 5%: Reserve Fund

7. Revenue Model

  • Transaction Fee: 1.5% per sale
  • Subscription Plans: $29/month & $99/month (premium analytics, ads)
  • Ad Placement: Promoted storefronts/products
  • Token Utility: Buyback & burn model tied to platform profits

8. Roadmap

  • Q2 2025: MVP launch with early vendors onboarded
  • Q3 2025: Crypto payment gateway and staking
  • Q4 2025: Mobile app release (iOS & Android)
  • Q1 2026: Global vendor acquisition and affiliate program rollout
