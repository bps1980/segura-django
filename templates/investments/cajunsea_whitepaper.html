1. Introduction

CajunSea Goods is a decentralized multi-vendor marketplace designed to empower entrepreneurs and retailers through blockchain technology. Unlike traditional platforms, CajunSea emphasizes transparency, low fees, and crypto-native payments for both buyers and sellers.

2. Vision & Mission

Our mission is to create the leading decentralized marketplace where local brands meet global customers, powered by secure Web3 infrastructure and trustless smart contracts.

3. Platform Overview

CajunSea Goods allows vendors to manage storefronts, list products, and process payments in both fiat and crypto. Every transaction is recorded on-chain for full transparency and accountability.

4. Technology Stack

  • Frontend: JavaScript (React/Next.js)
  • Backend: Python (Django) + Node.js
  • Blockchain: Solidity smart contracts on Polygon/Ethereum
  • Payments: Stripe, Plaid, Coinbase Commerce
  • Hosting: DigitalOcean (Docker + Kubernetes)

5. Core Features

  • Multi-vendor onboarding with KYC/AML verification
  • Custom storefront management with analytics tools
  • Smart contract-enabled transactions
  • Stablecoin and fiat support integration
  • Affiliate and ambassador reward programs

6. Tokenomics

The CajunSea ecosystem may be powered by the CJN Token (subject to final confirmation). Vendors who stake CJN receive discounts, premium placements, and voting rights for platform governance.

  • 40% – Community & Ecosystem Growth
  • 25% – Founders & Team (Vested)
  • 20% – Strategic Investors
  • 10% – Marketing & Partnerships
  • 5% – Reserve Fund

7. Revenue Model

  • Transaction Fees: 1.5% per sale
  • Vendor Subscriptions: $29/mo & $99/mo (premium analytics, ads)
  • Paid Ad Placement for storefronts/products
  • Token Utility: Buyback and burn model linked to platform revenue

8. Roadmap

  • Q2 2025: MVP Launch with Early Vendor Onboarding
  • Q3 2025: Launch Crypto Gateway & Token Staking
  • Q4 2025: Mobile App Launch (iOS & Android)
  • Q1 2026: Global Expansion + Affiliate Program Rollout
