GovTech Proposal Engine

Automating Government Bidding. Empowering Small Businesses.

Problem

  • Government contracting is complex and time-consuming.
  • Small businesses lack resources to compete effectively.
  • Manual proposal processes lead to costly errors and missed deadlines.

Solution

The GovTech Proposal Engine automates the identification, preparation, and submission of federal and state proposals using AI and predefined templates tailored to NAICS codes.

Key Features

  • Automated solicitation monitoring (SAM.gov, NASA SEWP, etc.)
  • Smart document extraction and scope analysis
  • Pre-built technical proposal templates
  • One-click generation of PDF submissions
  • Integrated email dispatch and follow-up tracker

Market Opportunity

  • $700B+ U.S. federal contract spending annually
  • Over 30 million small businesses eligible to bid
  • Only 5% currently engage with government RFPs

Revenue Model

  • Subscription tiers: Starter, Pro, and Enterprise
  • Pay-per-submission for non-subscribers
  • White-label licensing for consulting firms

Traction

  • Currently used internally by Segura Mgmt to submit multiple proposals
  • Automated 80% of manual proposal tasks
  • Actively bidding on $25M+ in government work

Tech Stack

  • Backend: Django, PostgreSQL, Celery
  • Frontend: React.js with Tailwind CSS
  • AI: GPT API for scope breakdown and custom prompt chains
  • Integration: SAM.gov API, PDFKit, SMTP
