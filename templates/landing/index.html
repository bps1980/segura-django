F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Key answers for new investors, partners, and early platform users.

After signing up and verifying your identity, you'll unlock access to detailed project pitch decks and whitepapers. You can invest directly from your dashboard using Stripe, Plaid, or crypto.

Minimum investments typically start at $1,000. Maximums vary per project and may be capped based on total raise limits or regulatory guidelines.

Project types include token-based investments, convertible equity, and SAFE agreements. Each project clearly outlines investor rights, returns, and funding structure.

Founders determine valuation based on market comparables, product development, intellectual property, and future growth. We provide full transparency per project.

You'll receive project updates, performance metrics, and investor documents through your dashboard. Some projects may include governance or voting rights.

Most investments are held until a liquidity event (such as acquisition or token listing). Token-based projects may support early exits via crypto exchanges.
